Our purpose at Whitewater Rafting is to help everyone experience an exciting and safe adventure on the rapids! Add one of our experiences to your next outdoor expedition.

History

Whitewater Rafting was founded in 1988 by John and Joanna Doe when they realized that their passion for river rafting could be used to show other people why the outdoors should be treasured. Since then, they have been river evangelists.
